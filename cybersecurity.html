Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Cyber Security internship program

01

Why is cyber security important?

▲

Cyber security has become crucial in today's digital world as organizations face increasing threats from hackers, malware, and data breaches. With the rise in cyber attacks targeting businesses of all sizes, protecting sensitive information, intellectual property, and customer data is essential. Cyber security professionals help organizations implement protective measures, detect threats, and respond to incidents effectively. Learning cyber security skills now positions you at the forefront of this critical field, opening doors to lucrative career opportunities and the ability to make a significant impact in protecting digital assets.

02

What are the career prospects in cyber security?

▼

The career prospects in cyber security are exceptional, with a global shortage of qualified professionals. Roles include Security Analyst, Penetration Tester, Security Engineer, Security Architect, CISO (Chief Information Security Officer), and Security Consultant. The average salary for cyber security professionals ranges from ₹5-30 lakhs per annum depending on experience and specialization. With organizations across industries increasingly investing in security infrastructure, the demand for skilled cyber security experts continues to grow, offering excellent job security and advancement opportunities.

03

Do I need prior experience in IT or programming?

▼

While some basic knowledge of IT concepts and programming is helpful, our program is designed to accommodate beginners. We start with the fundamentals and gradually build up to more complex security concepts. We provide additional resources and support for those who need to strengthen their technical skills during the program. The most important requirements are analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and a passion for learning about security.

04

Will I receive a certificate after completing the internship?

▼

Yes, upon successful completion of the internship program, you will receive three certificates: a course completion certificate, an internship certificate, and an outstanding performance certificate (based on your performance during the program). These certificates are recognized in the industry and will add value to your resume when applying for cyber security positions.

05

What kind of projects will I work on during the internship?

▼

During the internship, you'll work on a variety of hands-on projects that reflect real-world cyber security challenges. These include network security assessments, ethical hacking labs, security monitoring systems, incident response planning, security automation frameworks, and a comprehensive capstone project. All projects use real-world scenarios and are designed to build your portfolio and demonstrate your security skills to potential employers. You'll have the opportunity to work both individually and in teams, simulating the collaborative environment found in security operations centers.
