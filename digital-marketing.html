Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Digital Marketing internship program

01

Why is digital marketing important?

▲

Since the dawn of commerce, merchants have always tried to capitalize on the best possible market available to them. The rise of the online realm has given birth to the largest marketplace in human history, the internet. With the ability to remotely connect with billions of potential customers online, the need to learn how to properly sell your goods and services online is absolutely crucial to the continued success and growth of a company. Whether you are a fresher or even a tenured professional looking to diversify your skillset, or an entrepreneur looking to get the most out of his business, knowing the keys to success on the internet can open doors to higher sales, better salaries, and a better life.

02

What are the prospects of digital marketing as a career?

▼

Digital marketing offers excellent career prospects with diverse roles such as SEO Specialist, Social Media Manager, Content Marketer, PPC Expert, Email Marketing Specialist, and Digital Marketing Manager. The average salary for digital marketing professionals ranges from ₹4-15 lakhs per annum depending on experience and specialization. With businesses increasingly shifting online, the demand for skilled digital marketers continues to grow rapidly, offering job security and advancement opportunities.

03

Do I need prior experience in digital marketing?

▼

No prior experience is required. Our program is designed for beginners as well as those with some experience looking to enhance their skills. We start with the fundamentals and progressively move to advanced concepts, ensuring everyone can follow along regardless of their background.

04

Will I receive a certificate after completing the internship?

▼

Yes, upon successful completion of the internship program, you will receive three certificates: a course completion certificate, an internship certificate, and an outstanding performance certificate (based on your performance during the program). These certificates are recognized in the industry and will add value to your resume.

05

Will this program guarantee me a job?

▼

At Persevex we place an emphasis on equipping our interns with the practical skills necessary to succeed. This doesn't mean however, that the subject's theory is exempted from our course. We will teach you the necessary theoretical concepts and practical skills to succeed. We will also let you get hands-on experience from our top associated mentors who are distinguished in their respective fields.
