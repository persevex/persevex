Privacy Policy

Last updated on 08-05-2025

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing address, and payment information when you register for an account, make a purchase, or contact us.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage data through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Course & Learning Data: Your progress, quiz results, certifications, and feedback.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and manage our educational services
  • Process payments and complete transactions
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Communicate with you about your courses, account, and our services
  • Improve our website, services, and educational content
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who help us deliver our services, including payment processors, cloud hosting providers, and email service providers.
  • Educational Partners: When necessary to provide you with educational content or certifications.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Secure data storage practices

While we take all reasonable precautions, no online service is completely secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account information confidential.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@persevex.com.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and assist in our marketing efforts. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
