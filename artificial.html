Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Artificial Intelligence internship program

01

Why is artificial intelligence important?

▲

Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries across the globe by automating complex tasks, providing deeper insights from data, and enabling new capabilities that were previously impossible. From healthcare diagnostics to financial forecasting, AI is revolutionizing how businesses operate and how we interact with technology. Learning AI skills now puts you at the forefront of this technological revolution, opening doors to exciting career opportunities and the ability to create innovative solutions to real-world problems.

02

What are the career prospects in artificial intelligence?

▼

The career prospects in AI are exceptional, with demand far exceeding supply of qualified professionals. Roles include AI Engineer, Machine Learning Engineer, Data Scientist, Computer Vision Engineer, NLP Specialist, and AI Research Scientist. The average salary for AI professionals ranges from ₹6-30 lakhs per annum depending on experience and specialization, with senior roles commanding even higher compensation. As AI adoption continues to accelerate across industries, professionals with AI skills will remain highly sought after for the foreseeable future.

03

Do I need prior experience in programming or mathematics?

▼

While some basic programming knowledge (particularly Python) and mathematical understanding (especially statistics and linear algebra) are helpful, our program is designed to accommodate beginners. We start with the fundamentals and gradually build up to more complex concepts. We provide additional resources and support for those who need to strengthen their programming or math skills during the program. The most important requirements are curiosity, dedication, and a willingness to learn.

04

Will I receive a certificate after completing the internship?

▼

Yes, upon successful completion of the internship program, you will receive three certificates: a course completion certificate, an internship certificate, and an outstanding performance certificate (based on your performance during the program). These certificates are recognized in the industry and will add value to your resume when applying for AI-related positions.

05

What kind of projects will I work on during the internship?

▼

During the internship, you'll work on a variety of hands-on projects that reflect real-world AI applications. These include image recognition systems, natural language processing applications, predictive analytics models, recommendation systems, and a comprehensive capstone project. All projects are designed to build your portfolio and demonstrate your AI skills to potential employers. You'll have the opportunity to work both individually and in teams, simulating the collaborative environment found in the AI industry.
